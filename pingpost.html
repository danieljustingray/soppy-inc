PingPost

What is PingPost?

PingPost will be a Twitter-like social media channel. In PingPost (PP) you will be able to:

  • Chat with people
  • Make posts (called pings)
  • Post- and find work
  • Post your Current/status (what you're doing)

    • Your account will be fully customizable!
